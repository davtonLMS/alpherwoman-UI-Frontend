CHIOMA IFEANYI-EZE

Chioma is a brilliant accountant and entrepreneur who passionately loves small businesses.

She had an exciting 11-year working career, as an accountant, starting from PricewaterhouseCoopers, and going on head the finance/accounting functions in several organizations, including Jumia Nigeria.

In 2016, Chioma went on to launch Accounting hub, a tech-accounting firm, focused on serving accounting services to small business, using the most reachable delivery methods.

She built an online accounting shop which allowed businesses access to accounting services, by simply choosing the service they wanted and paying for it, like products.

Till date, accounting hub has served over 500 businesses and trained well over 3000 business owners.

Chioma says she is on a mission to reinvent the way that small business owners access help with their accounting and tax needs in Nigeria; using the power of story-telling and technology.

While learning to run Accounting hub, Chioma developed huge love for entrepreneurship and shares this with other people in business, as well as people looking to launch businesses, via her Facebook page, using the hashtag #chiomasbusinessschool.

She challenges old ways and believes there can always be a better way of doing things. She implements accounting systems for businesses in a record-period of 24 hours, she grew a massive stocktaking community of over 2000 people and automated the accounting system for over 250 businesses within 2 years.

Chioma is well-known as Nigeria's small business accountant and the tech-accountant.

She is a Chartered Accountant of 13 years, a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN), has a BSc in Accounting, an MSc in Accounting & Finance and and MBA. 

She is a recipient of several awards, both academic and entrepreneurial, as well as an amazing speaker and writer.

JUST IBE

Just Omomo Ibe is a “Sales Guru”. She is a self-motivated sales and marketing consultant with excellent communication skills. As the first point of contact, she engages with clients providing them expert advice. She approaches every customer with the sole purpose of serving their every need whilst making it a fun and enjoyable 5* experience.

Just Ibe is a Certified Management consultant and a London Graduate school specialist in Time Management. An Experienced banker with a known pedigree for turning around business situations. Working towards personal and team targets, she plays a key role in the success of my bank; developing and continually updating her product knowledge and expertise and sharing her passion about her fantastic products. She has bagged a number of awards for her contribution to entrepreneurship in Nigeria.

Just Ibe has authored 4 books aimed at showing entrepreneurs how to leverage digital media to maximize Sales. 

Her key competencies are creating sales and marketing strategies that produce a 360 degree turnaround in revenue and profit in addition to equipping employees of businesses with critical thinking skills to identify opportunities to grow.

Just Ibe has trained, empowered and coached over 1000 entrepreneurs across Africa, United Kingdom and USA all with a proven track record of visible business growth.

“Just Ibe runs three major businesses and recently launched a Tech company that is revolutionizing content creation and distribution in Africa and beyond. Her utmost desire is to help entrepreneurs in Africa and beyond leverage digital marketing to achieve exponential business results.”

EDACHE OBE

Edache Obe is a Digital Media Strategist who provides innovative digital media solutions for SME’s, SBM’s and large enterprises through digital media.

He is a graduate of Economics from the University of Jos Nigeria, he worked in the banking industry for 8 years which exposed him to different industries and levels of business stages, he learnt a lot of vital lessons operating a business, and those lessons he still carries till date.

After his exit from the banking industry in the year 2016, he kicked off his Digital and branding company that same year, Dacheo Media and Branding to provide digital media solutions.  Edache Obe has won the Unsung heroes award by Omaliving show and has also been reorganised by Business day Nigeria as one of the individuals that help promote SME’s and entrepreneurship.

He is currently working on a digital tool to help businesses maximize their investments through digital media processes.

He is a lover of food, traveling and education. He is also the founder of Lagos foodie association and Dacheo Media Academy.
