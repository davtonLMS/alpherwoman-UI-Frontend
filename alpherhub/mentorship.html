Mentorship

Welcome to Alpher Mentorship

Mentorship and sponsorship are key drivers of success, yet women can have a harder time finding mentors and sponsors, especially ones with influence. The good news is that we can mentor other women at any stage in our careers, and it pays off when we do. Women who are mentored by women feel more supported and are often more satisfied with their career.

Use our tips to be the best mentor/mentee you can be, and remember, like all good relationships, mentorship is a two-way street.

For Customers

Women, through their entrepreneurship and enterprise, make a substantial contribution to the economy and the society. In spite of their contributions, they face huge obstacles and challenges that severely limit their potential.
